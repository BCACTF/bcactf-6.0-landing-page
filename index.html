BCACTF 6.7

With great regret, we announce the cancellation of this year's BCACTF. We do not take for granted that players from around the world choose to spend their time on our challenges.

For this reason, we would rather host no CTF than an event that falls short of our high standard. We will be revisiting the planning and development of BCACTF to provide the experience that our community expects.

We sincerely apologize to all our players and hope to see you in next year's BCACTF.
